# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the ethical standards and behavioral expectations for all employees, contractors, and stakeholders of our organization.

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful workplace where everyone can contribute their best work without fear of harassment, discrimination, or retaliation.

## Expected Behavior

### Professional Standards

- Maintain the highest level of professional integrity in all business dealings
- Respect the dignity, rights, and contributions of all individuals
- Communicate honestly and transparently with colleagues and clients
- Take responsibility for your actions and decisions

### Workplace Conduct

- Treat all individuals with courtesy, respect, and fairness
- Foster an inclusive environment that values diversity
- Collaborate effectively and constructively with team members
- Maintain appropriate professional boundaries

### Confidentiality and Information Security

- Protect confidential and proprietary information
- Use company resources responsibly and for business purposes only
- Follow all data protection and privacy policies
- Report security incidents immediately

## Prohibited Behavior

### Harassment and Discrimination

- Any form of harassment based on race, gender, religion, age, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Bullying, intimidation, or threatening behavior
- Creating a hostile or offensive work environment
- Retaliation against individuals who report violations

### Conflicts of Interest

- Using company position for personal gain
- Accepting inappropriate gifts or favors from clients or vendors
- Engaging in activities that compete with company interests
- Failing to disclose potential conflicts of interest

### Misconduct

- Dishonesty, fraud, or misrepresentation
- Violation of laws, regulations, or company policies
- Substance abuse that affects work performance
- Misuse of company property or resources

## Reporting Violations

### How to Report

- Direct supervisor or manager
- Human Resources Department
- Anonymous ethics hotline: 1-800-ETHICS-1
- Online reporting portal: ethics.company.com

### Investigation Process

- All reports will be investigated promptly and thoroughly
- Confidentiality will be maintained to the extent possible
- No retaliation will be tolerated against good faith reporters
- Appropriate corrective action will be taken when violations are confirmed

## Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- Verbal or written warnings
- Mandatory training or counseling
- Suspension with or without pay
- Termination of employment
- Legal action when appropriate

## Resources and Support

### Training and Education

- Annual code of conduct training required for all employees
- Regular updates on policy changes and best practices
- Specialized training for managers and supervisors

### Support Services

- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Diversity and inclusion resources
- Mental health and wellness programs
- Legal and ethical consultation services

## Acknowledgment

By working for this organization, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with this Code of Conduct. You also understand that violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
